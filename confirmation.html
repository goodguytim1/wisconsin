Your Advocacy Package Is Ready

You now have everything you need to take a stand against unfair taxation and protect your livelihood. Remember, this isn’t just about Wisconsin. Should we fail, we can expect nearly every other state in the Union to follow suit, effectively ending the home-based travel agent industry.

To access the content, simply right-click the advocacy_package.zip file you just downloaded and select “Extract All.” In the folder that’s created, you’ll find three text files and four folders. This content is already customized with the correct names and contact information based on your address in Wisconsin.

In each of the four folders, you will see:

  • contact-info.txt — Contact details for the specific legislator or representative, including phone, address, online contact forms, and social media channels.
  • sample-email.txt — A ready-to-send email or letter, customized with the legislator’s correct name and title.

We recommend reaching out via both email and snail mail. We also strongly encourage phone calls and social media posts to amplify your message. And please remember to send a follow-up email if you don’t hear back within 2-3 weeks.

Yes, we’re asking you to send 4 emails, 4 letters, at least 1 call, and 1 social media post. We understand it’s a bit more effort, but it should only take 10 to 15 minutes — and that small effort will help protect the home-based travel industry in Wisconsin.

In the coming weeks and months, we will begin a media blitz across several Wisconsin cities and host webinars for the home-based travel agent community. Your calls, letters, and emails will be critical in raising awareness and affecting positive change!

Thank you for standing up and making your voice heard!
